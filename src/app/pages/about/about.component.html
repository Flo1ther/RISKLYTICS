About Stock & Crypto Risk Analyzer

The Stock & Crypto Risk Analyzer is a lightweight, fully frontend Angular application designed to help users evaluate and compare the risk levels of various financial assets, including both stocks and cryptocurrencies.

Key Features

  • Live price data visualization for both stocks and cryptocurrencies.
  • Dynamic volatility and risk index calculations.
  • Basic trend and correlation analysis between multiple assets.
  • Light and dark theme support (can be toggled in settings).
  • All data is fetched from public APIs â€” no backend or user accounts required.

Purpose

This tool aims to help investors make informed decisions by offering an accessible way to visualize asset trends, volatility, and comparative risks in real time. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced trader, this analyzer gives you a clear picture of market dynamics in one place.

Data Sources

Data is fetched in real-time from publicly available APIs such as Alpha Vantage, CoinGecko, and others.
